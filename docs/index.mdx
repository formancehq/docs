# Numary Ledger Developer Documentation

## What is Numary Ledger?

Numary Ledger lets you model and record complex money movements, helping you accelerate your business operations. Build financial dashboards, deliver payments to your marketplace partners, and accurately track revenue across your entire operation.

## Getting help

Are you stuck? Have a technical question not well answered in these docs? Run into a problem you can't solve?

Come chat with us! We have [a Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/xyHvcbzk4w) where we and others using Numary Ledger hang out.

Or, if you prefer, you can also [Tweet at us](https://twitter.com/numaryhq).

### I found a bug!

Did you find a bug in Numary Ledger? That's great! We need your help to make it better. You can [file a bug report](https://github.com/numary/ledger/issues/new?assignees=&labels=bug&template=bug_report.md&title=) and tell us all about it.

## Numary Ledger is Open Source

Numary Ledger is [Open Source](https://github.com/numary/ledger/blob/main/LICENSE), and we welcome your contributions, whether it's code or working on these very documents. We maintain a welcoming and inclusive community: [Join us on GitHub](https://github.com/numary/ledger) and learn how to get involved.
